Valence Electrons

Valence electrons are the electrons located in the outermost shell of an atom. To achieve stability, they must have around 8 valence electrons--or 2 for smaller atoms.

Momentum

Describes an obbject's "quantity of motion". It is a vector quantity with a unit of kg X m/s.

Force

Force is an external factor that can change an object's state of motion, or direction.
